Scott Designs Services

Scott Designs provides digital artwork in a wide variety of mediums for our clients. Established in 1932, our company's mission statement has stayed the same: "We produce images that people are addicted to". Since then our team of 10 people have strived to follow that mission statement with everything we do.

  • We have been in business since 1932
  • We are known for our designs of coloring animals unnatural colors
  • We won the "Peter Gregory" award in 1943 for our design "Mauve Horse"
  • We have filed both Chapter 7 and 11 bankruptcy
  • We once again apologize for our designs being used by the Soviet Union
